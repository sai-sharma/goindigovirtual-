Who Are We?

Oman Air Virtual is a community-driven virtual airline that simulates the operations of Oman Air. We provide a realistic and immersive experience for aviation enthusiasts, offering a wide range of routes, aircraft, and training programs.

About Us

Founded in February 2025 by Sai Sharma, Oman Air Virtual aims to bring the thrill of aviation to virtual skies. With a focus on realism and community, we offer a platform for pilots to grow, learn, and connect.

The Start

Founded by Sai Sharma | EmiratesPilot9 in February 2025, Oman Air Virtual has grown into a thriving community of aviation enthusiasts.

Explore The World From Oman

With 38 destinations across 23 countries, we connect Muscat to the world, offering a vast choice of routes for our pilots.

Ranking Structure

Our pilots progress through ranks, starting as Cadet Pilots and advancing to Senior Captains, unlocking new opportunities along the way.

Ranking System
