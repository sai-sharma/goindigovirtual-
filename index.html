Welcome to Oman Air Virtual! Explore our virtual airline and join our community of aviation enthusiasts.

Oman Air Virtual is a community-driven virtual airline that simulates the operations of Oman Air. We provide a realistic and immersive experience for aviation enthusiasts, offering a wide range of routes, aircraft, and training programs.

About Us

Founded in February 2025 by Sai Sharma, Oman Air Virtual aims to bring the thrill of aviation to virtual skies. With a focus on realism and community, we offer a platform for pilots to grow, learn, and connect.

Fleet

Explore our modern fleet of aircraft, designed for comfort and efficiency.

Boeing 787-8

The Boeing 787-8 is our flagship aircraft for long-haul routes. It offers exceptional fuel efficiency, passenger comfort, and a range of up to 7,530 nautical miles.

Airbus A330-300

The Airbus A330-300 is perfect for medium to long-haul routes. With a capacity of up to 300 passengers, it combines efficiency, comfort, and reliability.

Boeing 737 Max 8

The Boeing 737 Max 8 is ideal for short to medium-haul routes. It features advanced aerodynamics, fuel efficiency, and a range of up to 3,550 nautical miles.

Cadet Pilot Program

The Cadet Pilot Program at Oman Air Virtual ensures that every aspiring pilot gets the chance to fly with us.

Guaranteed Entry

If you apply for the Cadet Pilot Program, you are 100% guaranteed to start flying with Oman Air Virtual.

Eligibility

This program is available for all members with 0-10 flight hours.

Complimentary Cadet Program

If a pilot fails the main pilot application test, they will be automatically enrolled in the complimentary Cadet Program to improve their skills. This ensures that every pilot has a fair chance to progress and join our ranks.

How to Get Started?
