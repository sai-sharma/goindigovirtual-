About Us

At IndiGo Virtual, we are not just another virtual airline; we are a thriving community of aviation enthusiasts committed to replicating the operations of one of India’s largest airlines, IndiGo Airlines.

Our Fleet

IndiGo Virtual operates a fleet of highly advanced, modern aircraft that closely replicate the real-world fleet of IndiGo Airlines.

Airbus A320

The core of IndiGo Virtual’s fleet, ideal for short-to-medium-haul operations.

Airbus A321

Larger version of the A320, offering extended range and increased capacity.

Airbus A320neo

Environmentally friendly version of the A320, with quieter engines and better fuel efficiency.

Hubs

Ranks

Our rank system is designed to recognize the skills and dedication of our pilots while providing a clear path for progression.

Trainee

Starting rank for all new pilots. Complete training and submit your first PIREP to advance.

First Officer (FO)

Actively fly with IndiGo Virtual and log flight hours to gain experience.

Captain (CPT)

Experienced pilots trusted to fly domestic and international routes.
