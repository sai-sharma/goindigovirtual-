2. Pilot Operations

Pilot Ranks & Progression

  • Cadet Pilot: 0-10 hours
  • Second Officer: 10-30 hours
  • First Officer: 30-75 hours
  • Senior First Officer: 75-150 hours
  • Captain: 150-300 hours
  • Senior Captain: 300+ hours

Flight Regulations

  • All flights must be logged using Infinite Flight PIREP System.
  • Pilots must follow real-world flight plans and ATC procedures.
  • Minimum activity: 1 flight per month to maintain active status.

3. Fleet & Route Management

Fleet

  • Boeing 787-8: Long Haul
  • Boeing 737 MAX: Medium Haul
  • Airbus A330-300: Regional/International

Route Updates

  • Route Manager will update the database monthly to match real-world operations.
  • Seasonal routes will be adjusted based on demand.

4. Events & Community Engagement

  • Weekly Events: Organized flights for the VA members.
  • Partnership Events: Collaborations with other VAs for special routes.
  • Challenges & Rewards: Monthly pilot awards for best performance.

5. Training & Pilot Development

Training Programs

  • New pilots must complete an initial check flight.
  • Advanced training for ATC communication and long-haul flights.

Workshops & Guides

  • Regular updates on flight procedures.

6. Website & Technology Management

  • Website Updates: Monthly maintenance and improvements.
  • Pilot Roster System: Automated logging for flight hours.
  • Discord/Forum Management: Active communication for members.

7. Growth & Future Plans

  • Expand the route network based on pilot preferences.
  • Partner with more VAs for codeshare agreements.
  • Introduce AI-based flight tracking for better operations.
