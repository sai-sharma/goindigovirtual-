About Us

A lot of interesting routes, from the near Salalah to the far Kuala Lumpur. Of course, all these routes will be operated by our fantastic planes: from the tiny E175 to the fantastic B787–8.

The Start

This Virtual Airline was founded by Sai Sharma | EmiratesPilot9 in February 2025, with the help of some dedicated individuals.

Explore The World From Oman

With us, some 38 destinations across 23 different countries, we offer a vast choice of routes for our pilots, connecting Europe and Asia to Oman.

Ranking Structure

We offer our pilots a system of rank by aircraft, starting with the small ERJ–175 and progressing to the command of a Boeing 787–8.
